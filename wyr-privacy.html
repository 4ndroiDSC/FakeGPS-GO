Privacy Policy

Welcome to Would You Rather? app, developed by 4ndroiDSC. Below, we explain how we handle your privacy when using our application.

1. Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect or store any personal information on our servers. However, we use third-party services like Firebase Authentication and Google AdMob, which may collect data for certain purposes.

Firebase Authentication (Anonymous Mode)

The app uses Firebase Authentication in anonymous mode, which allows users to log in without providing personal information such as name or email. Firebase may generate an anonymous user ID, but it is not linked to personally identifiable information.

2. AdMob and Data Collection

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the app. AdMob may collect certain information, including device identifiers, cookies, and usage data, to provide personalized or non-personalized ads based on your activity within the app. For more information on how AdMob collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

AdMob and other third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information about users for advertising purposes.

4. User Control Over Data

You can opt out of personalized ads by following the instructions provided in your device's settings or through the Google Ads settings at Google Ads Settings.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users within the app when appropriate.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us via email at: 4ndroidsc@gmail.com.
